AVIGAN® / REEQONUS™ (Favipiravir)

AVIGAN® / REEQONUS™ (Favipiravir) is a broad-spectrum oral antiviral originally developed by FUJIFILM Toyama Chemical Co., Ltd. (FFTC) and approved in Japan as a treatment and stockpile countermeasure for pandemic influenza. Favipiravir disrupts viral replication by acting directly on the viral RNA polymerase enzyme.

Favipiravir has been used extensively in many countries since the start of the pandemic, either as an approved drug or as a compassionate treatment. More than half million people have been treated with Favipiravir, either in the generic form or in the original Avigan formulation.
